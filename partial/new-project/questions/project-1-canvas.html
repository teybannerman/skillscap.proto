As opposed to an elaborate business plan which limits your flexibility and openness to learning, a 'business model canvas' lays out your assumptions to be tested and describes how your company creates, delivers and captures value. It is flexible and will probably evolve as time passes and new ideas spring to mind. Whether your goal is to make money, get users, or grow traffic, your business model canvas describes how all of the pieces in your business interconnect.
